Emily Goes Undefeated to Win the Club All Play All

Emily Asquith has had an outstanding first season at the club, becoming a regular attendee and joining the team for the Cumbria League. But perhaps most impressively, she has won her first club All Play All tournament, which we host every season.

Not only that, she has proven unbeatable - with an impressive record of 4 wins and 2 draws.

A fascinating game against John Dobson () led to the two agreeing to a draw in an endgame where black (played by Emily) had winning chances - but after a gruelling 3 hour ordeal, no one can blame her for taking the draw.

She also beat longstanding members Dave Shaw, Jonathan Rashleigh, and Tim Lounds. The game against Tim () was decided by a devastating kingside attack by Emily with the white pieces - though a missed mate in 2 gave Tim a chance to force a draw!

Emily will be going on to play in the major section of the Durham Chess Congress this May, and we wish Kendal's newest rising star all the best!
