Welcome to Kendal Chess Club

About Us

We are a chess club that meets in the historic market town of Kendal, Cumbria. We welcome all comers from beginner to Grandmaster.


The club was revived after 15 years of inactivity in 1995 by our current chairman Malcolm Walker along with Dave Shaw, who still regularly plays for the club.

Since then, the club has played in the Cumbria League every year, finding good results and quickly rising to the First Division.


We are proudly affiliated with the Cumbria Chess Association and play in the Cumbria Chess League.

Meeting Info

We meet every Thursday from the beginning of September to the end of April from 7pm-10pm, at the Abbot Hall Social Centre on Dowker's Lane.

Free parking is available after 6pm at the Peppercorn Lane Car Park, just behind the centre.


There's something for everyone, with friendly games played every week, intra-club competitions, and league matches against other teams in Cumbria.
