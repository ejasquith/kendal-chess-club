Welcome to Kendal Chess Club

About Us

We are a chess club that meets in the historic market town of Kendal, Cumbria. We welcome all comers from beginner to Grandmaster.

We are proudly affiliated with the Cumbria Chess Association and play in the Cumbria Chess League.

Meeting Info

We meet every Thursday from the beginning of September to the end of April from 7pm-10pm, at the Abbot Hall Social Centre.
