Our History

Unfortunately, not much is known about the Kendal Chess Club before 1938! The records of the club and the Westmorland Chess Association it belonged to are lost to the annals of history.

What we do know is that in 1938, the Westmorland Chess Association merged with the Cumberland Chess Association, because of a request by the Kendal club to participate in Cumberland tournaments.
